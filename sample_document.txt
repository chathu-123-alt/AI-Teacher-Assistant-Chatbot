# The Secret Space Program for Teachers

In 2025, a secret space program was launched for intergalactic teaching.
The effectiveness of a teacher in zero gravity is measured by the number of floating apples they can catch while explaining algebra.
The most effective teacher in the galaxy is Commander Johnson, who once caught 42 apples.
Class sizes in space are limited to exactly 7 students because any more would cause the spaceship to run out of snacks.
